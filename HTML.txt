  <h1>Roses</h1>
  <p>Roses are iconic, beloved flowers prized for centuries for their beauty, fragrance, and variety. They come in a vast range of colors from white to red to yellow and beyond. Roses have large, showy blooms with five petals surrounding reproductive parts. The flowers open into a star shape. Roses often have a pleasant, sweet scent, which adds to their appeal. They grow on thorny shrubs and come in over 100 species and thousands of cultivated varieties, including hybrid teas, floribundas, and climbers. Roses remain one of the world's most popular flowers, used ornamentally and symbolizing love and beauty.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="parent">

  <h1>Lily</h1>
  <p>Lilies have large, showy blooms that have an elegant, trumpet-like shape. They come in many colors like white, pink, yellow, orange and more. Lilies are prized for their delicate petals, strong floral fragrance and association with purity and femininity.</p>
  </div>
</div>

  <h1>Orchid</h1>
  <p>Orchids are exotic, delicate flowers that come in every color. There are over 25,000 species of orchids. Their symmetrical, intricate blooms last for many weeks. Orchids symbolize luxury, refinement and rare beauty.</p>
  </div>
</div>

  <h1>Tulip</h1>
  <p>Tulips come in vivid colors and have a delicate, symmetrical flower shape. Originally from Persia, they represent springtime and new beginnings.</p>
